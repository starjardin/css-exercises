    • transition-timing-function
  • Difference beteween timing-function, delay, and duration:
    • delay: The trandition delay secifies the duration to wait for the affect of the property to change if when its value changes
    • timing-function: The transition-timing-function propetry sets how inermediate values are calculated.
    • duration: sets the length of time being desplayed during the transition
